About Me

Hello, my name is Yuki and I’m a professionally trained baker that graduated from culinary school in Japan over twenty years ago. Over that time I’ve taken the time to craft the perfect recipes, influenced from my time in multiple bakeries, to scratch that sweet tooth. Because of that, I hope you’ll take the time to consider ordering from me!

- Yuki
